Nipsey Hussle

Born on August 15th, 1985 in South Central, Los Angeles, California. Ermias Joseph Asghedom was a father, business man, rapper, and an activist for the betterment of his community and people. He was assasinated on March 31st, 2019.

Through his various mixtape releases. Nipsey Hussle was able to fuel his brand TMC (The Marathon Cotinues) which led to him opening his own clothing store which helped him fund his first studio album. The Victory Lap. His album received rave reviews and lead him to his first ever Grammy Award.
